Happiness is contagious

Where employees and customers fall love

By Dan Armstrong

This is an intro, keep it or not

Imagine a company where employees enjoy coming to work. When they arrive, they find that the customers are happy to be there too. Isn't that a company you would expect to grow and prosper?

There are such companies. The opposite exists as well - organizations where employees suffer through the day, and resentful customers get out as fast as they can.

How to tell which is which? Go to the companies that make it their business to ask - places that survey employees ( Glassdoor , Comparably and CareerBliss ) . Check out sites that survey customers as well. And to tell which companies are thriving, look at standard financial metrics like revenue and profit growth.
