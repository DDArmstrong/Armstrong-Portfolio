BFFs of the power elite: The John Malone Mafia

By Dan Armstrong

Skills: Python, scraping, BeautifulSoup, Pandas, Observable

I was curious about the demographics and relationships among directors on corporate boards. For almost every publicly traded company in the world, Reuters maintains a page listing each director, their age, position, and when they were appointed to the board. I obtained a list of companies in the Russell 3,000 - the top 3,000 US public companies by capitalization - and went to work scraping the information about the directors.

The result was a dataset of about 45,000 names - with quite a few surprises. A 20-something running a bong empire. A 97-year-old CEO, son of a Lithuanian coal miner. And by finding the names that appeared most often, I found a group of men running a half-dozen companies by sitting on each other’s boards.

It’s the John Malone mafia….(to be continued).
